Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon teapowder
 - 1 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
  1. In a bowl, add half cup water and sugar.
  2. Heat it until its boiled then add 1 teaspoon sugar and tea powder.
  3. Heat it all together for around 10-15 minutes and tea will be ready to drink.
